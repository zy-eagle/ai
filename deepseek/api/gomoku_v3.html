How to Play

Gomoku, also known as Five in a Row, is a classic strategy game where two players alternate placing stones on a grid.

  • Black plays first, then players alternate turns
  • The goal is to be the first to get five stones of your color in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
  • Click on any empty intersection to place your stone
  • The game ends when one player achieves five in a row or when the board is full

This ancient game originated in China over 2,000 years ago and remains popular today for its simple rules but deep strategy.
